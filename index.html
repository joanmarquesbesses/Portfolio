Warhammer 40,000: Blood and Thunder

A 3D fast-paced top-down action game inspired by the gameplay style of Diablo and set in the grimdark universe of Warhammer 40,000. Face relentless hordes of enemies, fight across large outdoor battlefields, the claustrophobic interiors of a space cruiser, and a deadly boss arena. Blood and Thunder was developed using our custom engine, Hawk Engine, built entirely by our programming team.
